Pizza Sales Project in MySQL

The Pizza Sales project utilizes a structured dataset to manage and analyze various aspects of pizzeria operations, including pizza types, sizes, ingredients, categories, and customer orders. This data enables the pizzeria to track customer preferences, optimize inventory, and analyze sales trends. The project categorizes pizzas by type and size, details the ingredients used, and records customer orders with information on the type and size of pizza, order date, and total price. This comprehensive data collection supports strategic decision-making, helping to improve menu offerings, manage inventory effectively, and tailor marketing strategies to enhance sales performance.
